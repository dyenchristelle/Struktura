At Struktura, we value your trust. Protecting your personal information and ensuring secure transactions are top priorities. We use reliable technologies and follow strict protocols to keep your data safe from unauthorized access.

Payment Protection

All online transactions are processed through trusted and secure payment gateways. We never store your credit card or banking details on our servers. Every payment is verified and encrypted to protect your financial data.

Account Safety

We encourage customers to create strong passwords and never share them. Our system automatically locks accounts after repeated failed login attempts to prevent unauthorized access.

Data Privacy

Your personal information is collected only for order processing, delivery, and support. We do not sell or share your information with third parties without your consent. For more information, please read our Privacy Policy.

How You Can Help

  • Never share your login credentials.
  • Beware of phishing emails pretending to be from Struktura.
  • Contact us immediately if you notice suspicious activity on your account.

If you have any security concerns or notice something unusual, please reach out to us at security@struktura.com.
