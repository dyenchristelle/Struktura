Return and Refund Policy

We value your satisfaction. Learn how Struktura handles returns and refunds.

1. Eligibility for Returns

We accept returns for items that are defective, damaged, or incorrect upon delivery. To be eligible, the item must be unused and in its original packaging.

2. Return Period

Customers have 7 days from the date of delivery to request a return. Requests made beyond this period may not be accepted.

3. Return Process

To initiate a return, please contact our support team at info@struktura.com with your order number, photos of the item, and a brief explanation of the issue.

4. Refund Process

Once your returned item is received and inspected, we will notify you via email. Approved refunds will be processed within 5-10 business days to your original payment method.

5. Non-Returnable Items

Customized or clearance items cannot be returned unless they arrive damaged or defective. Please confirm details before placing your order.

6. Shipping Costs

Return shipping fees are the responsibility of the customer unless the return is due to a Struktura error (e.g., wrong or defective item).

7. Exchanges

We currently do not support direct exchanges. Please return the item for a refund and place a new order for the replacement product.
