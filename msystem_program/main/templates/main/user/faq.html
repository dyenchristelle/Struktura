Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers about our furniture, delivery, and services.

What materials do you use for your furniture?

We use high-quality wood, metal, and upholstery materials sourced from trusted suppliers to ensure durability and comfort.

Can I customize the color or size of furniture?

Yes! Struktura offers customization for most pieces. You can request color, size, and finish adjustments depending on availability.

How can I check the status of my order?

After placing your order, you’ll receive an email confirmation. You can track your order through your Struktura account or contact our support team.

Do you deliver outside Laguna?

Yes, we offer nationwide delivery within the Philippines. Delivery fees and times may vary based on your location.

What should I do if my order arrives damaged?

Please contact us immediately with photos of the damaged item. We’ll arrange for repair, replacement, or refund depending on the issue.
