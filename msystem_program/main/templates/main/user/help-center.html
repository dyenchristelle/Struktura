Help Center

Find answers to common questions or get in touch with Struktura’s support team.

How can I track my furniture order?

Once your order is shipped, we’ll send you a tracking number via email. You can also log in to your Struktura account and check the “My Orders” section for updates.

What is your return and exchange policy?

We offer a 7-day return or exchange policy for items in original condition. Simply contact us through our support form with your order details to start the process.

Do you offer custom furniture designs?

Yes, Struktura offers made-to-order pieces tailored to your space. Our team will help you choose materials, finishes, and dimensions that suit your preferences.

How long does delivery usually take?

Delivery typically takes 5–10 business days, depending on your location and order size. Custom items may take 3–4 weeks to craft and deliver.

Can I visit your showroom?

Absolutely! You can visit our showroom in San Pablo City. Please schedule your visit in advance so our design consultants can assist you.

Still have a question?

Send us your concern and we’ll get back to you shortly.
